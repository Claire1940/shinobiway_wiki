A support healer still needs threat presence. You are not a passive health station.

Use jump-cancel style movement (chakra jump cancel timing) to stay mobile and harder to lock down. Practice this in a low-pressure mode until it becomes automatic.

| Skill | Input Focus | Match Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Jump-cancel rhythm** | Chain landing into immediate re-jump | Reduces stationary vulnerability |
| **Lateral repositioning** | Move side-to-side before healing | Harder to burst by ranged focus |
| **Short dive-in/out pokes** | Enter for chip, leave before punish | Adds pressure without overcommit |

### Block pressure concept

Healer heavy attacks can create openings against defenders who rely on guarding. Start offense with a safe heavy check, then take short confirms and disengage. Your goal is not max combo damage; your goal is disrupting enemy timing while preserving your cooldown cycle.
