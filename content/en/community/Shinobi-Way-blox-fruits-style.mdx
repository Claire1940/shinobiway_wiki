## Core Stat Allocation for the Shinobi Way

To truly excel in the **Shinobi Way blox fruits style**, your stat points must be allocated with extreme intentionality. As established by top-tier players, you receive three stat points per level, and how you distribute these determines your "main damage" source. For a shinobi, this is typically split between Melee, Defense, and either Sword or Blox Fruit.

In 2026, the most effective ninja builds avoid "hybrid" distributions. Splitting points across four or five categories weakens your overall potential. Instead, focus on a "Tri-Stat" build that maximizes your survivability and your primary killing tool.

| Stat Category | Priority | Benefit for Shinobi |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Melee** | **High** | Increases Energy (Stamina) for dashes and skills. |
| **Defense** | **High** | Increases Health to survive high-damage combos. |
| **Sword** | **Primary** | The core damage source for the "True Shinobi" build. |
| **Blox Fruit** | **Secondary** | Used for utility or stuns if Sword is the primary. |
| **Gun** | **Low** | Generally ignored in this specific playstyle. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are using a sword like the Cursed Dual Katana as your primary damage source, do not put points into Blox Fruit. Use your fruit abilities only for movement or stunning.

## Fruit Synergy: Choosing Your Utility

While the sword is your primary weapon, your Blox Fruit acts as your "jutsu." In the **Shinobi Way blox fruits style**, you aren't necessarily looking for the fruit with the biggest explosions, but the one that offers the most control over the battlefield.

*   **Ice Fruit:** Excellent for freezing enemies in place, allowing for a guaranteed sword combo. The "Ice Skating" ability also adds to the theme of high-speed movement.
*   **Portal Fruit:** The ultimate choice for a "Space-Time Shinobi." Being able to teleport instantly and create parallel dimensions makes you nearly impossible to track.
*   **Shadow Fruit:** Fits the aesthetic perfectly. Use the darkness to obscure your movements and drain the life force of your foes.
*   **Dough Fruit (Awakened):** While often used by many builds, its "sticky" stuns allow a shinobi to land complex sword maneuvers with ease.

## Advanced Combat Tactics and Combos

Mastering the **Shinobi Way blox fruits style** requires more than just holding a sword; it requires understanding the "Combo Meta." A typical ninja engagement follows a specific rhythm: Stun, Close Gap, Burst Damage, and Retreat.

### The "Flash Strike" Combo Template
1.  **Initiation:** Use a long-range stun (e.g., Ice V or Portal Z).
2.  **Movement:** Use your Flash Step (Racial ability or basic teleport) to get behind the enemy.
3.  **Sword Skill 1:** Apply immediate pressure (e.g., Yama Z).
4.  **Fighting Style Transition:** Use a move like Godhuman C to keep the enemy airborne.
5.  **Finisher:** Use your primary sword's high-damage skill (e.g., CDK X).

> **Warning:** Never use all your energy in one combo. Always keep at least 1,000 energy reserved for escaping if the enemy uses a Ken Trick or escapes your stun.
