## Efficient Clearing Strategies

To maximize your time in the **Shinobi Way missions**, you should utilize a team built for speed rather than pure defensive utility. Since the difficulty curve is relatively forgiving compared to Roundup Missions or Team Attack Missions, focus on units with high movement speed and large Area of Effect (AOE) Ninjutsu.

### Recommended Team Composition
1. **The Lead (AOE Specialist):** Use a unit like Seventh Hokage Naruto or Sasuke (Wandering Ninja) who can clear entire rooms with a single skill.
2. **The Support (Healer/Buffer):** While rarely needed in early chapters, a healer ensures you maintain the health threshold required for 3-star mission completion.
3. **The Boss Killer:** A unit with a high-damage single-target Ultimate (EX Ultimate) to quickly end the final encounter of each stage.

### Utilizing Auto-Battle
If your team's total Power (POW) is significantly higher than the recommended stage power, enable the "Auto" and "Auto-Skill" buttons. This allows you to progress through the **Shinobi Way missions** while focusing on other tasks. However, be wary in Chapters 18 through 20, as the AI may occasionally walk into traps or fail to dodge powerful boss Ultimates, potentially costing you the 3-star completion reward.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I farm the Shinobi Way missions multiple times for Shinobites?**

A: No, the primary Shinobite rewards are tied to first-time completion and specific mission goals. Once a stage is "3-starred," you will only receive standard loot like Hero Fragments or materials on subsequent runs.

**Q: What is the fastest way to clear all 20 chapters?**

A: The fastest method is to use a high-POW team with AOE capabilities and set the game to Auto-Battle. Focus on units that have "Dash" skills to move between rooms quickly. Most players can finish the entire set of **Shinobi Way missions** in about an hour.

**Q: I missed some Shinobites in Chapter 10. Can I go back?**

A: Yes, you can revisit any chapter at any time. Check the mission select screen for stages that do not have three gold stars; these are the ones where you still have rewards left to claim.

**Q: Are there any secret rewards in the Shinobi Way?**

A: While there are no "hidden" stages, completing certain milestones in the Shinobi Way triggers "Normal Achievements" in the main menu, which provide extra rewards like Ninja Frog cards used for leveling up Ninjutsu.
