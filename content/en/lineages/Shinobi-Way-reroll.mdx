## Getting Started: The Foundation of Rerolling

Before you can efficiently farm rerolls, you must establish a strong foundation for your character. Rerolling in Shinobi Way is divided into three primary categories: Clan, Race, and Trade. Each requires a specific type of token, but the farming methods for all three often overlap.

The most common mistake beginners make is attempting to farm rerolls before they have unlocked the necessary passives. To truly excel, you need to reach a specific power threshold that allows you to automate the process.

### Leveling and Passive Shards
Your first objective is to reach Level 10,000. While this may sound daunting to new players, the current 2026 meta allows most players to achieve this in under an hour by farming high-yield mobs. Once you hit this milestone, you unlock the ability to use Spec Passives, which are critical for increasing your drop rates.

| Requirement | Objective | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 10,000** | Unlock Spec Passives | Essential for Fortune and Damage buffs |
| **Passive Shards** | Farm at Judgment Island | Used to roll for specific sword traits |

> **Warning:** Do not spend your passive shards on low-tier weapons. Save them for endgame swords like the True Manipulator to ensure your investment scales with your progress.

## The Infinite Tower: The Gold Standard for Rerolls

While farming mobs on various islands can yield some results, the Infinite Tower is the undisputed king of reroll farming in 2026. The logic of the tower is simple: the higher the floor you reach, the better the rewards.

### How to Access and Automate
You will need Tower Keys to enter, which can be gathered in bulk from Judgment Island. Once inside, the goal is to reach at least Floor 70. At these higher tiers, the game begins awarding multiple clan and trade rerolls per floor. 

1. **Enter the Tower:** Use a key and start the session.
2. **Initial Delay:** Some players prefer to wait out the first few minutes to synchronize their auto-clickers.
3. **Activate Auto-Replay:** Use the in-game "Pay and Replay" feature to keep the session going indefinitely.
4. **Skills Management:** Ensure your strongest AOE (Area of Effect) skills are set to auto-cast.

### Recommended Melee Styles
For the Infinite Tower, your choice of melee style is vital. You need a balance of high damage and wide range to clear floors before the timer expires.
*   **Shinobi Melee:** Currently the top choice due to its massive raw damage output. It scales exceptionally well with the Strongest Shinobi passive.
*   **Gilgamesh Melee:** An excellent alternative that offers superior crowd control, making it safer for players with slightly lower defensive stats.

> **Tip:** If you are farming overnight, ensure your PC or mobile device has "Auto-Replay" enabled and that you have enough currency to cover the replay costs. A 5-hour session can yield upwards of 8,000 clan rerolls if optimized correctly.

## Advanced AFK Strategies

For veteran players, the best way to accumulate a **Shinobi Way reroll** surplus is through multi-accounting. By using an emulator to run multiple accounts simultaneously, you can farm the Infinite Tower in a party.

### Using Alt Accounts
When you bring alt accounts into the Infinite Tower, they act as "anchors" and additional loot collectors. Even if the alt accounts are not dealing damage, they still receive rewards for every floor the main account clears. This effectively doubles or triples your total reroll gain per hour.

### The Abyssal Outfit
While farming the tower, keep an eye out for the Abyssal Outfit. This accessory is one of the best in the game, surpassed only by the Atomic Outfit. It provides significant stat boosts that make high-floor farming much easier for newer players who haven't yet maxed out their primordial runes.

## Troubleshooting Common Farming Issues

Even with the best setup, you may encounter obstacles in your farming journey. Here is how to handle the most common problems:

*   **Low Reward Yield:** If you are getting fewer than 5 rerolls per floor, you likely need to push to a higher floor. Rewards significantly increase after Floor 50 and peak around Floor 100.
*   **Dying Early:** If your character cannot survive the mobs, focus on obtaining the Abyssal Outfit or switching to the Gilgamesh melee for better survivability.
*   **Running Out of Keys:** Spend 30 minutes at Judgment Island specifically farming bosses to replenish your Tower Key stock for the week.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a Shinobi Way reroll for my clan?**

A: The fastest method is farming the Infinite Tower. By reaching floors 70-100, you can earn between 7 and 11 clan rerolls per floor. Combining this with a 2x Drop Gamepass and the Fortune passive will maximize your results.

**Q: Can I farm rerolls if I am below level 10,000?**

A: While you can participate in some activities, it is highly recommended to hit level 10,000 first. This unlocks the Spec Passives like Fortune, which are necessary to make the farming process efficient. Without these, your drop rates will be significantly lower.

**Q: Is the "Shinjuku Method" still good in 2026?**

A: The Shinjuku method (using Quake and standing still) is generally considered the slowest way to farm rerolls. It is only useful if you are specifically looking for Aura Crates. For clan, race, and trade rerolls, the Infinite Tower is much superior.

**Q: Which melee is better for AFK farming: Shinobi or Gilgamesh?**

A: Shinobi is currently the better option for pure damage, allowing you to clear floors faster. However, Gilgamesh is a great secondary choice if you need more utility or if you haven't fully optimized your damage stats yet.
