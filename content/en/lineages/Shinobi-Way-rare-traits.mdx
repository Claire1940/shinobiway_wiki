## FAQ

**Q: Can I have more than one trait active at a time?**

A: By default, every shinobi starts with one trait slot. However, as you reach higher levels (Level 50 and Level 100), you can unlock a second and third slot. This allows you to stack multiple **Shinobi Way rare traits** for devastating combo potential.

**Q: Are Mythical traits always better than Legendary ones?**

A: Not necessarily. While Mythical traits have higher raw stats, some Legendary traits have "utility" effects that are better for specific game modes. For example, the **Healer's Touch** (Legendary) is far superior for Raid content than the **Destroyer's Eye** (Mythical), which is purely focused on solo PvP damage.

By leaving your character in the meditation zone while you are away from your computer, you earn 1 spin every 15 minutes. This is the preferred method for many players to accumulate the hundreds of spins needed for Mythical **Shinobi Way rare traits**.
